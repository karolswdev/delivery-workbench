# Delivery Workbench - Brand Notes

**Repository name:** `delivery-workbench`

## Positioning

Delivery Workbench is an evidence-first operating framework for agentic
software delivery: roadmap structure, commit-time contracts, evidence pairing,
daily work logs, deferred summarization, and mid-project adoption discovery.

## Visual Direction

The icon should feel like a precise builder's bench for software delivery:
practical, technical, durable, and slightly playful. The retro-computing signal
is intentionally subtle: early desktop-era restraint, crisp pixels, muted
blue-grey surfaces, warm amber accents, clean black/white contrast, and
workbench-like composition.

Avoid direct references to any specific historical computer brand, mascot,
logo, ball motif, or trademark. People who know the era may feel the influence;
nobody else needs to be told.

## Suggested Taglines

- Evidence-first delivery for agentic software work.
- Roadmaps, commit contracts, evidence, and work logs for AI-assisted teams.
- A senior architect's workbench for shipping with proof.
